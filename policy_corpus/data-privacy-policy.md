# Data Privacy Policy

**Version:** 1.1  
**Date:** March 10, 2026  
**Compliant with:** Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023, GAID 2025  

## 1. Introduction
NSR is committed to protecting personal data in line with NDPA and GAID requirements.

## 2. Data Collected
- Personal: Name, email, phone, age, gender  
- Preference: Budget (NGN), location (state/LGA), amenities, travel purpose  
- Technical: IP, device, usage logs  

## 3. Lawful Basis
- Consent (explicit for sensitive inferences)  
- Contract (for booking facilitation)  
- Legitimate interest (service improvement)  

## 4. User Rights
Access, rectification, erasure, objection, data portability (per NDPA Section 36+).

## 5. Security Measures
- Encryption (TLS 1.3, AES-256 at rest)  
- Access controls (RBAC)  
- Regular DPIAs for major processing  

## 6. Sharing
Hotels receive only necessary data post-consent. No sale of data.

## 7. Data Retention
User profiles: 3 years post-last activity; logs: 12 months.

## 8. Breach Notification
Notify NDPC and users within 72 hours if high risk.
