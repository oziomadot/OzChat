# NaijaStay Recommender (NSR) – Company Overview and Mission Statement

**Document Title:** Company Overview and Mission Statement  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** March 10, 2026  
**Approved by:** Board of Directors, NSR  

## 1. Introduction
NaijaStay Recommender (NSR) is a technology-driven platform that provides personalized hotel recommendations and booking assistance to individuals seeking accommodation across Nigeria.

## 2. Mission
To empower every traveler in Nigeria with intelligent, secure, and culturally sensitive hotel recommendations that match their location, budget, age, gender, and personal preferences.

## 3. Vision
To become the most trusted hotel recommendation engine in Nigeria by 2030, leveraging ethical AI to enhance travel experiences while protecting user privacy.

## 4. Core Values
- User-Centricity  
- Privacy & Security First  
- Inclusivity & Cultural Respect  
- Transparency  
- Innovation with Responsibility  

## 5. Business Model
NSR operates a freemium web and mobile app platform. Users input preferences (location, age range, gender considerations, budget in NGN, amenities like family-friendly, business, women-only facilities, etc.). An AI-based recommendation engine suggests hotels from partner listings. NSR earns commissions on facilitated bookings via integrated payment gateways.

## 6. Key Stakeholders
- Users (individuals seeking lodging)  
- Hotel partners  
- Employees & contractors  
- Regulators (NDPC, CAC, etc.)

All policies in this corpus derive from and support this mission. See Data Privacy Policy and Diversity & Inclusion Policy for related commitments.
